🔒 Privacy, Terms & GDPR Notice

1. Local-Only Processing

BlackBox is a fully client-side, in-browser application. All uploaded files (Excel, CSV, Word, JSON, TXT and unstructured data) are parsed, analysed, summarised and visualised entirely within your browser. No file content, no parsed data, no derived analytics, and no metadata is transmitted to any external server, cloud service, or third party at any point.

2. Hosting Model

The tool can be hosted on your own infrastructure, on an internal intranet, or run directly from a local HTML file. There is no telemetry, no analytics SDK, no tracking cookies, and no remote logging. Your data never leaves the device on which the browser is running.

3. Optional AI Chat Feature

If — and only if — you explicitly enable the AI Chat module and configure your own LLM API key in Settings, the natural-language prompts you type (and a derived schema/summary of the active dataset) are sent to the LLM provider you selected, under your own contract and credentials with that provider. BlackBox itself does not store, intercept, or forward this traffic. Disabling AI Chat returns the tool to fully offline operation.

4. No Data Sharing

No data is shared between BlackBox and any party other than the user. There is no shared backend, no multi-tenant database, no analytics provider, and no advertising network involved.

5. GDPR Compliance — Your Rights

BlackBox is designed to support compliance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (the General Data Protection Regulation). Because processing happens locally on the data subject's device, the user remains the sole controller of their personal data. The following rights are operationally supported within the tool:

  • Article 15 — Right of access: all stored data is viewable in the "Raw Data" tab and exportable from the Privacy & GDPR settings panel.
  • Article 16 — Right to rectification: data files may be replaced or re-uploaded at any time.
  • Article 17 — Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): use the "Wipe All Data" action in Settings → Privacy & GDPR to permanently delete every uploaded file, derived analysis, chat history, configuration value, and local-storage entry from your browser. This action is irreversible.
  • Article 20 — Right to data portability: use "Export All Data" in Settings → Privacy & GDPR to download a portable copy of all data held by the tool.
  • Article 25 — Data protection by design and by default: the architecture itself prevents server-side processing; minimisation is enforced by design.
  • Article 32 — Security of processing: data never leaves the browser sandbox, eliminating transmission-layer risk.
