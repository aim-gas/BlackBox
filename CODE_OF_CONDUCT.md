# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behaviour: empathy and kindness, respect for differing opinions, constructive feedback, accepting responsibility, focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour: sexualised language or imagery, trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, harassment, publishing others' private information, other inappropriate conduct.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. Reports of abusive behaviour may be sent privately to the maintainers (or gas.ac). All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.
